Switzerland

Switzerland is a dreamland of alpine mountains, beautiful lakes, and charming villages. I enjoyed hiking in the Swiss Alps, tasting delicious Swiss chocolate, and exploring cities like Zurich and Lucerne.

Brussels

Brussels, the heart of Europe, is known for its stunning architecture, waffles, and chocolates. The Grand Place was mesmerizing, and I loved walking around the city’s historic streets and trying local food.

Lisbon

Lisbon is a city of hills, trams, and colorful buildings. I enjoyed the view from Miradouros, listened to Fado music, and savored delicious Pastéis de Nata. The energy and charm of Lisbon are unforgettable!
